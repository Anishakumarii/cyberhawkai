Financial Fraud (UPI/Bank)

  • Step 1: Call 1930 immediately to block the transaction.
  • Step 2: Call your bank's customer care to block your card/account.
  • Step 3: Do not delete any SMS or transaction proofs.
  • Step 4: Use our FIR Generator to draft a complaint.

Hacked Social Media

  • Step 1: Try to reset your password via email/phone if still accessible.
  • Step 2: Warn your friends/family not to accept requests or money demands.
  • Step 3: Report the compromised account through the platform's official help center.

Lost / Stolen Mobile

  • Step 1: Call your telecom operator to block the SIM card immediately.
  • Step 2: Block your UPI apps by calling your bank.
  • Step 3: Register a complaint on the CEIR portal to block the device IMEI.
